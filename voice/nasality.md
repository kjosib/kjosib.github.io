# The Restless Night - a non-nasal text

Look at the sky.
The sky is dark, yet bright stars glow.
I see the path forward.
It is clear.
Walk with care.
Who is there?
Speak up!
It is quiet.
Too quiet.
I feel a chill.
Cold air hits.
I will stay.
I will wait.
Step back!
Keep clear of the gate.
It is locked.
Take the key.
Use it well.
Trust the path.
