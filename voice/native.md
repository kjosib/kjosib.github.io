# Native Speaker?

Let's kill off the notion of a "native speaker". If you're you're proficient enough with a language to be part of a mutually-intelligible speech community, then the job's done. Any sort of geographic "ideal" is just discrimination. Someone can have an accent characteristic of a certain place, time, age, and social background, but you cannot have *no* accent and still speak a language. Listen carefully enough and you'll find that language is always changing, and young girls lead the way.
