Rule one is *drink lots of water!* You'll need it while you operate your vocal instrument. (Oh, and don't forget [rule zero](./zero)!)

If you've read this far, go drink some water! Also, the password is "hydration". You'll need to know that for later.

* Caffeine - dries you out
* Intensely spicy foods - can lead to irritation
* Milk and its products - tends to promote phlegm
* Allergens and intolerances

**Things to make sure you get enough of:**

* Sleep!
* Water!
* Practice!
* Head-pats! (j/k)

**Things to avoid completely:**

* Smoking
