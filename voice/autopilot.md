# Auto-Pilot

If your trans voice:

* passes when you're paying attention to it, but tends to falter otherwise, or
* passes to other people, but still feels inauthentic, or
* is consistent when you read a standard text, but falters at other times,

then chances are you need to re-tune your vocal auto-pilot.

The more automatic your trans-voice is, the more natural it will sound to your own ears
and the more consistently you'll perform in stressful circumstances.

To change the default settings in your vocal auto-pilot,
you'l need to find the point where it begins to kick in,
and then reset your voice back to your preferred configuration.
Keep doing this over and over for long enough,
and your automatic behaviors will adjust to your new intention.
Not only that, but you'll sound more natural to yourself,
since it's the result of *not fighting habit* anymore.

If you want to find your breaking point, you're going to have to push yourself.
Generally this means speaking off the cuff and responding to challenges.
In rougly increasing order of difficulty:

* Introduce yourself.
* Talk about the weather lately.
* Narrate the facts of some recent or current events in your city or state.
* Tell your weekend plans. How did you decide? How long have you been planning this?
* Tell us about your interests and hobbies. What got you into this? What's your next investment of time or money?
* Tell a story about something good that happened, or something important to you.
* Give an expressive monologue as if you were auditioning for a play.
* Take a position for or against free will (or some other eternal question) and explain your reasoning.
* Explain a problem you're having at work. Why is it a problem? What caused it? How do you plan to address it?
* Give a critical analysis of recent political developments, either at home or abroad.
* Tell a story about a time you or a loved one suffered grievously.
* Justify a controversial and unpopular opinion that you hold.
* Give a job interview.
* Defend a masters' thesis.
* Start a new religion and gain 1,000 converts.
* Negotiate a hostage situation.
* Negotiate peace in the Middle East.
* Eradicate a type of parasite from the Earth.
* End world hunger.
* Talk your boss into giving you a meaningful raise.

Each of these will work your conscious mind and emotional state harder than the last, forcing your voice to go on autopilot. Does it wobble or break? When? How? When did you notice? (Are you recording this?) What does it take to recover?
